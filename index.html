What is this?

Build a website that allows users to register with their name, password, and age. When a user logs in, show them a bar chart histogram of all the ages of everyone who has registered. For example, if three people have registered, aged 30, 38, and 30, the chart should would two bars; one of 30 at height 2, and one of 38 at height 1. Highlight the bar of the logged in user in a different color than any of the other bars.

This website is built from a GitHub respository to meet the goals of the assignment above. It uses a jsonblob.com store as its data store and renders the chart using d3. The chart is basically a copy-paste of this block.
